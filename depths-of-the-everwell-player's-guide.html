Depths of the Everwell Player's Guide

The Astral Rings, looming high above the skies of Kalcrea, have grown lively over the last few days, their entrancing lights dancing eagerly along their intersecting orbits. A pale green glow alight with ribbons of vibrant blue dominates the rings. In their readings of the complex patterns and motions of the Astral Rings, the Astraseers of Kel Soren claim that a Season of Flux is imminent. Being the first Season of Flux of this Succession, it's coming will mark the Spark of First Refuge for the residents of Kel Soren, a celebration which honors the town's founder, Ilithi, and her perilous flight from the Black Crescent Vale over 2000 years ago.

As residents of Kel Soren, your group is busy preparing to embark upon a pilgrimage which often marks one's first steps beyond the confines of Kel Soren's marbled walls and its surrounding forest, The Whispering Pines. Pilgrims depart from Kel Soren upon the dawn of the Spark of the First Flux, which is expected to arrive at the end this Span, and travel north until they reach the southern edge of the Black Crescent Vale. There, the pilgrims make camp for the night and then return to Kel Soren the next day. They must arrive before the gates of Kel Soren close to consider their pilgrimage a success. To remain true to Ilithi's journey, pilgrims bring only their clothes, a single waterskin, a small backpack which they can fill with whatever they desire, and a single tool or weapon of their choice. They are also encouraged to perform the pilgrimage in small groups in order to foster the spirit of community and selflessness that Ilithi embodied.

Your mentors are running you through your last tasks and drills to ensure you are ready to take on this long journey; being so close to the Aetherborn Confluence means that the Season of Flux can be quite dangerous, and there will be much ground to cover.
